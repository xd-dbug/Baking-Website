How to Order

Here's what to expect when you place a custom order with Sealed in Sugar.

1

Reach out

Contact me via email, Instagram, or Discord with your idea — occasion, flavours, rough size, and your event date. The more detail the better, but don't worry if you're not sure yet.

2

We discuss your vision

I'll get back to you within 48 hours to talk through design options, flavours, dietary needs, and anything else that'll make your order perfect.

3

Receive a custom quote

Once I understand what you need, I'll send a detailed quote. Pricing is based on size, complexity, and ingredients.

4

Confirm & pay deposit

To secure your date, I'll ask for a 50% deposit upfront. The balance is due on collection or delivery. Your order isn't confirmed until the deposit is received.

5

Pick up or delivery

On the agreed date, collect your order from [Your City] or arrange local delivery. I'll make sure everything is packaged safely.

Please note: I typically need at least 2 weeks notice for most orders, and 4+ weeks for large or complex cakes. The earlier you reach out, the better!
